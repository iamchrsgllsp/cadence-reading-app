Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

At Cadence, we believe your reading journey is personal. This policy outlines how we handle your data with respect and transparency.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the Cadence experience:

  • Account Data: Email and display name provided during signup.
  • Library Data: Books tracked, reading progress, and curated collections.
  • Interaction Data: Technical logs (IP addresses) for security and performance monitoring.

2. Data Storage & Security

We use Supabase for authentication and database management. Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We never sell your personal information to third-party advertisers.

3. Your Rights

You have the right to access, export, or delete your account data at any time. To request a full data deletion, please contact our support team.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in. By using Cadence, you consent to these functional cookies.
