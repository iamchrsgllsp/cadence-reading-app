Ongoing Behind The Scenes

Everythings an RPG

  • Levelling system

    Gain experience points for every book you complete, level your profile, unlock badges and get access to new features.

  • Badges and Roles

    Gain badges for completing reading challenges, hitting milestones, and more. Show off your reading achievements on your profile.
    View roles of users when they run a book club, host discussions, or contribute to the community in other ways.

Wider Community Features

  • Bookclubs

    Join or create book clubs. Moderate discussions, set current book for everyone each month, schedule meetups and more.

  • Import and Export CSV

    Easily import data from other platforms and export your Cadence data for other platforms or your own analysis.

  • Offline app functionality

    Update book progress and status even offline, sync when you're back online.

  • Social Sharing

    Customisable shareable graphics to show off your reading stats, current reads, badges to share easily on social media or with friends.

Q2 2026

Community Update

  • Friend Profiles

    View your friends' books, add them to your TBR, and start discussions on your favorites.

  • Friend Reading

    See what friends are also reading the same book and compare stats on the fly. This is currently showing on android app to bug test along with friends system and so you can get a feel for it - let us know if you want this visual turned off for now.

  • Messages

    Send your friends messages or share new book discoveries directly.

Better stats, recommendations and global features

  • Multi-Format Tracking

    Track progress for Audio, Kindle, and Hardcover books.

  • It's in the details

    Better stats including genre breakdowns, reading pace, and more to help you understand your reading habits and preferences.

  • Global Recommendations

    Get personalized book suggestions based on your reading history as well as trending books in the Cadence community.
